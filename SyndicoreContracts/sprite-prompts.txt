================================================================================
  SYNDICORE — SPRITE GENERATION PROMPTS (v2)
================================================================================

Svi prompt-ovi za Tripo3D Image Generation (Nano Banana Pro ili GPT Image 1.5).
Svi su ispod 1000 char-a.

================================================================================
  WORKFLOW (za svaki sprite)
================================================================================

  1. U Tripo Image Gen panelu, upload tvoj ODOBRENI HQ (hq_pyramid_v1.png)
     kao REFERENCE IMAGE. To je style anchor za sve preostale sprajtove.
  2. Izaberi model: GPT Image 1.5 ili Nano Banana Pro (šta god bolje radi
     za tvoj konkretan HQ look).
  3. Paste prompt (iz ovog fajla).
  4. Aspect ratio: 1:1.
  5. Number of images: 1 (ili vise ako zelis da biras).
  6. Generate.
  7. Download PNG.
  8. Background removal: remove.bg ili Photoshop magic wand (crno → transparent).
  9. Drop u Xcode → Assets.xcassets/<asset_name>_v1.imageset/
  10. Run app → Settings → Debug → Sprite Alignment Test.
  11. Izaberi building type, podesi 5 slidera (Anchor X/Y, Scale, Rotation, Zoom)
      dok se plinth sprajta ne poklopi sa cyan/orange debug outline-om.
  12. "Copy to clipboard" → paste u SpriteCatalog.swift kao novi `static let`.
  13. Dodaj case u `SpriteCatalog.spec(for:)` switch.
